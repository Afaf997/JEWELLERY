About Us

We are one of the oldest business families in India with a family legacy of 112 years in business. Started for the noble cause of nation-building and self-sustenance in a pre-independent India, the forefathers believed ethical, honest and transparent business practices should form the foundation of the group. Having ventured into jewellery retailing in 1993 in Thrissur, Kerala, India, where we are headquartered, and as on June 30, 2020, we have 137 showrooms spread across India and GCC
